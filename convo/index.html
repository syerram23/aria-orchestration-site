FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ARIA Talk.

What does “Replace Your Meeting” actually mean?
It means exactly what it says. If you have a meeting that follows a structure — screening, qualification, onboarding, training, check-ins, research — ARIA Talk's AI agent has that meeting for you. You define what you need covered, share a link, and the AI handles the live voice or video conversation. You get scored summaries, transcripts, and structured data — without ever being in the meeting.
How is this different from a chatbot?
Chatbots handle text-based Q&A with predefined decision trees. ARIA Talk conducts full voice and video conversations with adaptive, context-aware follow-ups. It feels like talking to a real person, not clicking through a script. The AI listens, understands nuance, asks intelligent follow-up questions, and produces structured insights that go far beyond what any chatbot can deliver.
How much does ARIA Talk cost?
ARIA Talk starts at $79/month per seat, which includes 20 minutes of conversation time. Additional minutes are billed at $0.10/min. Enterprise plans with unlimited conversations and custom pricing are available for larger teams. There are no setup fees, no long-term contracts, and you can start with a free trial.
Is my conversation data secure?
Absolutely. ARIA Talk is SOC 2 Type II certified and fully compliant with GDPR and CCPA. All conversations are encrypted end-to-end, both in transit and at rest. Your data is never used to train AI models. We offer data residency options for both EU and US, and enterprise customers can configure custom data retention policies.
How long does it take to set up?
Most teams are up and running within 15 minutes. Choose a use case template, customize your questions and scoring criteria, and you are ready to share your first link. No technical setup, no code, no IT involvement required. Enterprise deployments with custom integrations typically take 1-2 weeks.
What integrations does ARIA Talk support?
ARIA Talk integrates with your existing tools via webhooks, allowing you to push conversation results to any system. We support direct integrations with popular ATS platforms, CRMs, and project management tools. Enterprise plans include custom API access and dedicated integration support. Native integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, and Workday are on our roadmap.
What kinds of meetings can ARIA Talk replace?
ARIA Talk replaces six types of meetings out of the box: screening interviews, discovery/qualification calls, intake and onboarding meetings, training sessions, stand-ups and check-ins, and customer research interviews. Each comes with pre-built templates you can customize. You can also create custom flows for any structured meeting your team is tired of having.
Can participants use ARIA Talk on mobile devices?
Yes. ARIA Talk works entirely in the browser with no app downloads required. Participants simply click the link you share and the conversation begins immediately. It works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices across all major browsers, making it accessible to anyone, anywhere.

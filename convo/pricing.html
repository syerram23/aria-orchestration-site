LLM Credits

How LLM Credits Work

ARIA Talk uses a simple credit system tied to active AI conversation time. No complex token math.

Included Minutes

Each seat includes 20 minutes per month of active AI conversation time. This covers the AI speaking, listening, and processing in real time.

Overage Pricing

If you exceed your included minutes, overage is billed at $0.10 per minute, charged at the end of each billing cycle. No surprises.

Enterprise Volume

Enterprise customers get custom volume pricing with unlimited minutes and dedicated rate cards tailored to your usage patterns.

Monthly Reset

Credits reset every billing cycle. Unused minutes do not roll over. Usage is tracked in real time from your dashboard.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as conversation time?
Only active AI speaking and listening time counts toward your minutes. Setup time, idle time between responses, and time spent reviewing results do not count. You only pay for the moments the AI is actively engaged in conversation.
Can I change plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you will get immediate access to the new plan features.
What happens if I exceed my minutes?
If you go over your included 20 minutes, overage is billed at $0.10 per minute. Overage charges are calculated and billed at the end of each monthly billing cycle. You can monitor your usage in real time from the dashboard.
Do you offer annual billing?
Yes, we offer annual billing with a 20% discount. Pay annually and your effective rate drops significantly. Contact us or select annual billing during checkout.
What's included in the free trial?
The free trial gives you 14 days of full access to all Get Started features, including all 6 use cases, voice/video/text conversations, scored summaries, and transcripts. No credit card is required to start.
How does Enterprise pricing work?
Enterprise pricing is volume-based and custom to your organization's needs. We work with your team to understand usage patterns, seat count, and integration requirements, then build a custom pricing package. Talk to our sales team to get started.

How LLM Credits Work

Included Minutes

Each plan includes a set amount of AI conversation time per seat per month. Only active AI time counts — setup, idle time, and configuration don't use credits.

Overage Rates

If you exceed your included minutes, overage is billed at $0.10/minute. Enterprise plans include volume pricing for high-usage teams. No surprises — you can set usage alerts.

Monthly Reset

Credits reset at the start of each billing cycle. Unused credits don't roll over. Upgrade anytime to increase your allocation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as AI conversation time?

Only active AI speaking and listening time is counted. Setup, configuration, idle time, and review of results don't consume minutes. A typical screening conversation uses 8-12 minutes.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. No penalties for switching.

What happens if I exceed my included minutes?

Overage is billed at $0.10 per minute at the end of the month. You can set usage alerts to avoid surprises. Enterprise plans offer custom volume pricing.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes. Annual plans receive a 20% discount. Contact sales for annual pricing on Professional and Enterprise plans.

What's included in the 14-day free trial?

Full access to all features in your selected plan. No credit card required to start. At the end of the trial, choose a plan to continue.

How does seat-based pricing work?

Each seat is one user with full access to their plan's features. All seats on an account must be on the same plan. Volume discounts available for 10+ seats.

What integrations are included?

Starter and Professional include standard integrations: Slack, Calendar, Webhooks, and common ATS/VMS connectors. Enterprise adds custom integrations with Workday, Salesforce, ServiceNow, and any system via API.

Is there a minimum commitment?

No. Monthly plans have no minimum commitment. Cancel anytime. Annual plans require a 12-month commitment with the 20% discount.
